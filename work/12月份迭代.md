#桌台情况报表
###VO属性 tradeTableSituation
``` java
areaName 区域
tableName 桌台
tradeCount 销售单数
receivableAmount 应收金额
discountAmount 折扣金额
afterDiscountAmount 折后金额

commercials 门店列表(查询条件)
startDate 开始日期(查询条件)
endDate 结束日期 (查询条件)
```

涉及的数据库表有:
trade_table 
trade
TABLES
commercial_area

```sql
    SELECT
        commercial_area.area_name areaName,
        trade_table.table_id tableId,
        trade_table.table_name tableName,

        count(trade.id) tradeCount,
        sum(trade.sale_amount) receivableAmount,
        sum(trade.privilege_amount) discountAmount,
        sum(trade.trade_amount) afterDiscountAmount
    FROM
        trade_table
    INNER JOIN trade 
        ON trade.id = trade_table.trade_id
        and trade.status_flag = 1 and trade.trade_type in (1,3,4) and trade.trade_status=4 and trade.trade_pay_status=3
    INNER JOIN TABLES ON TABLES .tableId = trade_table.table_id
    INNER JOIN commercial_area ON commercial_area.id = TABLES .areaId

    where trade_table.status_flag =1
    and trade_table.shop_identy in
```
